New Year's Day 2024

Auckland was the first major city to welcome 2025 with thousands of revellers counting down to the New Year and cheering at colourful fireworks launched from New Zealand’s tallest structure, the Sky Tower, and a spectacular downtown light show. Countries in the South Pacific are the first to ring in the New Year with midnight in New Zealand striking 18 hours before the ball drop in Times Square in New York. In Australia, fireworks blasted off the Sydney Harbour Bridge and across the bay to welcome the New Year. More than a million people had gathered at Sydney Harbour for the celebration featuring British pop star Robbie Williams, who led a singalong with the crowd. The upcoming Year of the Snake in the Asian zodiac is heralded as one of rebirth, alluding to the reptile’s shedding of its skin. Stores in Japan, which observes the zodiac cycle from January 1, have been selling tiny figures of smiling snakes and other snake-themed products. Other places in Asia will start marking the Year of the Snake later with the Lunar New Year.

2024 Winter Olympics in Italy

The 4th Winter Youth Olympic Games took place in the province of Gangwon in the Republic of Korea, which already hosted the Olympic Winter Games PyeongChang 2018. These first Winter YOG to be held in Asia build on the Olympic Games legacy and did engage even more young people through various activities before, throughout and after the Games. The vision of Gangwon 2024 was to let the youth celebrate peaceful coexistence and unity through sport to create a better future together. The aim was to drive positive change and social unity in the region, impacting society in the areas of sport, culture and education. Gangwon 2024 did benefit from many of the facilities used for PyeongChang 2018. This will ensured a cost-efficient and sustainable YOG, and, at the same time, provided the participating athletes with the opportunity to compete in some of the same sites as Olympians did a few years before.

2024 U.S. Presidential Election

Donald Trump will be America’s 47th president, after mounting the most momentous comeback in political history that will hand him massive, disruptive power at home and will send shockwaves around the world. Four years after leaving Washington as a pariah, following his attempt to overturn the 2020 election to stay in office, Trump’s victory defied two assassination attempts, two presidential impeachments, his criminal conviction and many other criminal charges.
