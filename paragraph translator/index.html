2.2.2 Features of Planning

The features of planning function of management are as follows:

Planning is a continuous process: The continuous feature of planning implies the relation of planning with planning cycle. A planning cycle implies the formation of a plan, implementation of the framed plan, and following up another plan, and so on. It is a continuous process because of the following reasons:

  • Planning function is never-ending, as it exists in a dynamic business environment.
  • Planning is done for a specific period of time and plans are reformed at the end of that specific period as per the new requirements and changing conditions.
  • Planning goes on, till the existence of an organisation, as issues and problems keep cropping up in the organisation and plans are needed to tackle the problems effectively.
